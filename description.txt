This mod simply adds new Spiky Ball variants.

These include:
 - Sticky Spiky Ball
 - Poisoned Spiky Ball
 - Bouncy Spiky Ball
 - Spiky Ichor Ball
 - Spiky Crystal Ball
 - Spiky Cursed Ball
 - Spiky Venom Ball
 - Spiky Beetle Ball

They can all be crafted on a workbech with the required materials.
